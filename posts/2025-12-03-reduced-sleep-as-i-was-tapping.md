## Today's Intention

Reduced sleep as I was tapping code into a computer module unit late last night. I wish I could programme in machine code, but I fear this is an art that will be lost to the gods soon enough. It will become such a abstract notion to think everything we do in the data industry is just a series of true and false. I guess the world is simpler down there. No grey area. You either are, or you are not. Just imagine the volume of bits that go into a question like, how do you feel? The shear amount of information required to calculate an answer to such a question can not be underestimated. So if you ask someone how they feel today, don't be to disappointed if they say. Ok. We are computationally bound systems and perhaps such a question was enough to overload our compute capacity. So be kind to those that don't say much. Maybe we are just processing the universe mindfully. And this takes time.
