## Today's Intention

There's a Honda engine in my garage that has been in pieces for three months. I bought it full of optimism and a YouTube education. I was going to restore it over a weekend.

That was foolish. And it was also exactly right.

Because what the engine has actually given me is not a working motorcycle. It has given me a lesson in patience that no podcast or book could have delivered. You cannot rush a Honda engine. It does not care about your timeline. It will reveal its problems in its own order.

My intention today is to take that same energy into everything else. What is asking me to slow down and look more carefully?

## I'm Grateful For

The smell of engine oil on my hands after an hour in the garage. It smells like honesty — like something real being worked on. No pretence. Just metal and effort.

## Something I'm Great At

I am great at staying curious when things break. Most people see a problem as a stop sign. I see it as a door that just needs a different key.
