## Today's Intention

The solutions we seek are just there. Open your eyes. You will be able to see them. And if you can't see them. Close your eyes, count to 10, take five deep breaths, stand on one foot for 15 seconds, open you mouth and make a LaaaaRRR sound for five seconds, look at something living, do a double thumbs up, lean back a little, smile and say Heeeeeyyyyyyy to that living thing. Then lets reset. Close those eyes. Count to ten. Keep them closed. One big breath in through the nose. Fill you chest. Maximum air. In In In In... And let it out with a BIG Aaaaarrrrrrrr as you open your eyes. Do you see it? Do you? Look. Look at it. The solution is starring you right in the face. Now merge with it and let this new knowledge guide you throughout the next minute, hour, day, week, month, year, lifetime.
