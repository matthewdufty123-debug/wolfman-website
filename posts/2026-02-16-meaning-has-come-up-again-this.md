## Today's Intention

Meaning has come up again this morning. I woke up with a fizzy headache, a nasty cough, and a moody brain. All I wanted was to start my morning routine, which begins with a coffee. And .... The kettle was broken. It was tripping out the electric. So... Like a the rugged wild wilderness man that I am, I got to rubbing tWo sticks together to make a fire and boil some water. And by that I mean, I put some water in a pan and waited for it to boil.

This process makes the stakes feel much more high. I was having to engage with the process much more closely. Monitor the water, make sure there was enough in the pan. Ensure it didn't spill. And wait. Wait patiently. The whole process felt like I was much more engaged with what it means to make a coffee. I felt like I was involved with whether this process was going to be successful or not. The hardship of having to make effort gave me greater meaning this morning.

So what did we learn? For me, this re-enforced a lesson I teach myself, my son and anyone that is willing to listen to me. Play life on HARD mode, even if easy mode is available to you. Proactively do things the HARD way, because things that are HARD to do, have much more meaning and deliver a greater sense of satisfaction.

Drive a old manual car with no driver aids, have face to face conversations about difficult subjects, make your coffee by boiling water the traditional way, and watch your life become filled with purpose 💗
