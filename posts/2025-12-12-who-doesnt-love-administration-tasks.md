## Today's Intention

Who doesn't love administration tasks. Lets do some administration today. Work admin, life admin. Whatever admin floats your boat. Lets get on it. It's one of those things we huff and puff about, but once your in it, and if you have ever completed a piece of admin, you will know it can be quite satisfying. A little dopamine hit as you tick off the task. Closing a ticket at work, shredding some paper at home. Gosh there must be quite a few different types of administration tasks we could start, progress and close off. Actually, just think about that for second. Think of all the possible administration tasks the universe has to offer, and imagine a dopamine hit value of 1 per task complete. How many potential feel good hits are there on offer from the realm we are navigating? More than one, and less than infinity depending on your belief system. So if your not feeling to great today and you want to lift your mood, do one or multiple administration tasks and feel the frown turn upside down.
