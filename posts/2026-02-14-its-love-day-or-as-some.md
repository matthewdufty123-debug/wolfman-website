## Today's Intention

It's Love Day, or as some call it, Valentine's Day. And on this occasion I think it would be apropos to talk about things I Love in the workplace.

And so... Things I Love about the workplace are;

- Playful mocking of peoples scripts. Peer Review is where we learn most. Having your colleges point and laugh and your script is a fun way to motivate yourself to do better next time. It's ok to write poor scripts. I do it all the time. We get struck in a thought hole, and it hard to see outside of a line of thinking. Sometimes it takes a college to say "Why do you write your procedure like that? Why didn't you just do this". Then you go.... "Oh yeah, Cheers Big Ears!"

- Working with the same colleagues that were shaming you about your script a minute a go, on a big project. I love working as a team to achieve a big goal. When your out of harmony with a team that is working towards a goal, things can feel like a grind. When your in harmony..! BooM The pace of work increases and the quality peaks. It's a beautiful place to be, but not easy to achieve. More times then not, you have to have petty arguments, and bruise each other's egos before you find the teams flow. To get there quickly, it best to foster a culture of openness and willingness to speak the truth and receive feedback that might make you uncomfortable. But... As long as the truth is grounded in Love, we can work with it. Then we become a team built on corporate Love. And they say Love is unbreakable.

## I'm Grateful For

Power BI. It's just better than the competition. I'm one of those people than attaches themselves to a brand and doesn't let go unless things become outwirte aweful. And I and firmly in the Power BI camp. Whatever you produce with lame tools like Tableau, I will do better with Power BI. And by better, I mean I think it's better.

## I'm Grateful For

Making a reporting dashboard that makes the end user feel Love for the world. Let's break this down... Makes them smile at how easy insights are to draw from the visuals. This ease makes them feel joy, makes their job easier, helps them get promoted, have more time to explore meaning, which further enables them to become a shining beacon of positive energy that will propegrate to family, friends and any one they interact with. Making the world incrementably a more Lovely place with each click of the dashboard I have built.
