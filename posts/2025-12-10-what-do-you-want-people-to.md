## Today's Intention

What do you want people to say about you after you have left a company? What is the sentence that people default to when describing your legend. I think that is a really important question you can use to challenge to daily actions. "He was a nice guy" what does that say about you professional tenure at a company? "They were so organised", "That person knew their sh!t", "They have annoying levels of attention to detail", "Golly, they were next level demanding, but wow, did we get some stuff done". These statements define us. Anyone external to ourselves can form these judgements. And there will be many different flavours along the way. Back to the question, what do we want people to say about you after you leave. What is the aggregate statement you want to stand supreme? Yes, you pissed some people off alone the way, yes you deployed a solution that created more problems that it solved. Those are all trailing edge statements (you hope....) . What stands upon the top of the bell. The Statement Supreme that is you. What will that chisel into the granite above reception for all to read as they pass through "Matthew Dufty, He was a f_ck!Ng great data engineer" I'd be happy with that.
