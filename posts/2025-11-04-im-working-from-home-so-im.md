## Today's Intention

I'm working from home so I'm going to have a great day writing a process in Pyspark language in Databricks. That's not a sentence I thought I would be saying a few months ago, but here I am. A Data Engineer, doing my Data Engineering tasks. It's all very exciting. I forecast I'll have minimal human contact today being at home and there are no meetings in my diary. A great day to get lost in flow with my code. Those that I do speak to today I'll make sure ever word counts and makes a difference. Redundant words take away from what we can possibly be, so let's make our words count today. Ever if there are only a few of them.

## I'm Grateful For

My immune system. This might feel like a bit of a flex (and it is). I have a wonderful immune system. Disease comes in and out of our body all the time. It's only when we let it manifest into something the disrupts our routine do we notice it. I keep my immune system in great condition through positive manifestation of a healthy body, a clean diet, lots of exercise and..! one of the most important elements. Cold water exposure. Cold shower every day, no days off. Today was a crazy day. I finished the cold shower, turned it off got out. Felt really buzzed up so I turned the cold back on and got back in for a minute. Not done that before so I feel like an extra strong warrior today with an amazing immune system.
