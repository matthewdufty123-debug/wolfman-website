## I'm Grateful For

Driving, I like to think I'm pretty good at driving. When I was very young, still in the teens, I had a driving job. Delivering stuff in a small rear wheel drive utility truck. It was super lite weight with nothing in the back. No traction control or any safety features to speak of. This was the 90's which feels like the wild west compared to today. Driving with "Spirit" meant you had to pay attention. If it was even remotely icy or just darn wet this truck would drift everywhere. So much fun. It was like being at a drivering school for 8 hours a day, every day. Well, not quite, but you know. It was fun. That experience inspired a series of rear wheel drive cars throughout my life. Driving such vehicles ingrained into my brain how to control a car in a slide. Now, I would never condone going out in the middle of the night in a old BMW 3 Series to practise sliding your car around. That would just be silly. But if you were forced to do such a thing because you partner was calling you a Chicken 🐔 for not doing it, and then, should a situation arise in the future where you did get yourself into a sticky spot, loosibg control of your car, you will be able to handle it much better having got some practise in first. Be safe out there 💗
