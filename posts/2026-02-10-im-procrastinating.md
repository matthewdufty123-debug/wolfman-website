## Today's Intention

I'm procrastinating. Making lots of noise in my head about upgrade my job title to have a "Senior" prefix. But not actually executing on the application process. So like most things in life that are difficult to achieve, it's time to make a plan and set a goal.

What's the Plan; "Apply for one senior position per week until I am success in a application."

What's the Goal; Let's do this properly...

"By the end of 2026 I will be in a role with a 'Senior' prefix on the title. I will be doing amazing work in the data and analytics space that is having a net positive impact on the world. I will be further increasing my knowledge / skills and leveling up those around me, plus anyone I am mentoring or leading. I'll make new friends, build my professional network, find greater meaning in work I am doing and I will increase my salary by 25%. Being in this position will bring more joy and greater purpose to my life. This increase in my happiness will propagate to those interact with in my personal life and most importantly, those I love."

## I'm Grateful For

The internet. It's really easy to find jobs being advertised on the internet.

## I'm Grateful For

Staying calm. I'm not someone that worries, even when faced with eminent doom. In fact, I believe this is where is thrive. When the road before you is burning and evil lurks round the corner. Or put on your fire suit, load up your blaster with as much ammunition as you can find, and head out down the hot dangerous road to face the perceived evil on your terms?

Maybe the evil you feared so much will become a business partner and you will end up making a data warehouse that serves a series of Power BI reports that makes the world a happier place? That, or you just zap the nasty orks with your zapper. Either way, I think you will have more fun walking forward than crying in the basement.
