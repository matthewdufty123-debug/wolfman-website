## Terminology

Always use **achievements** — not "rewards". Achievements are earned milestones, not prizes given.

- Earned milestones: e.g. 10 journals logged, 30-day streak, first ritual completed
- Streak tracking (built on the Statistics data layer)
- Badges displayed on user profile
- Achievements overview page: `/achievements`
