## Today's Intention

Fake it till you make it, they say... Follow your intuition... Believe in yourself... All fancy clothes to put around the words, I don't know what I'm doing. And with that, I'm delivering some training today. To say I don't know what I'm doing is being a bit overly dramatic. I do know this subject, but still, when you're delivering training to a group of professionals, imposter syndromes kicks in. Am I really got enough to do this? The answer is yes, of course you are otherwise you wouldn't be doing it, and if your not, just pretend you are, then you good enough even if your not. Either way it's a win. So here is to training day. A day we can all enjoy if we do or to not know what were being trained in or training.

(side note... I love the idea of the trainer not knowing what they're training and the people not knowing what they're being trained in. Imagine what wonderful things you would come out of the training session having learnt with zero agenda or expectations. We just learnt and learnt together...)
