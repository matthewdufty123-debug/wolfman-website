## Today's Intention

I have lost shight of my goal to move towards my next role this past month. I have been having so much fun in my current role , this goal escaped me. Time to reallign, refocus and manifest a future when I'm walking into a Senior position in a Data Department. Manifesting something is quite beautiful. Try it. Sit cross legged, back straight. Close your eyes and Live it, Be it and Become it.

## I'm Grateful For

I got a 1 month trial of Linkedin  premium the other day. It advertises increased visibility for recruiters and companies recruiting. This ties in nicely with speaking to people about senior data opportunities in the job market.

## I'm Grateful For

Scoping items. It's so important to clearly define what we intend to do before we set out on a tasks. What is equally as important is defining what we are not going to do. So much does expectation become a let-down when a person, or people, observe what your doing about protect a result. "Matthew is working on a Ice Cream Van Sales report, I can't wait to see the Sales figures." Actually... The sales report looks at missed sales opportunity while 'Ice Cream' Dave, is having a cigarette round the back of his van six time a day. Lets Make Scoping Cool Again. After which we can celebrate our scoping by scooping some of the delicious ice cream.
