## Today's Intention

There is a sense of calm and focus propagating across the earth today. I felt it seconds after I woke. Usually my brain is bombarded with two or three major things that need my attention. I use my morning routine to bring order to the chaos running through my mind. Butt... Today there was nothing of note. I sat and did my breath work and felt a feeling of peace inside my body. This is beautiful because it's going to enable me to really focus at work today. Solutions to techical problems will surface as easy as a buoy in the ocean. Conversations will flow with ease. And people will say, "well that was easy". Actually, it was always easy. We just thought it was hard, therefore, it was. So here is to a beautiful day. And... Golly. I really need to work on my core for Christmas. So its Core this evening in the gym.
