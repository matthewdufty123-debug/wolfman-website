## Today's Intention

Achievement is one of the most exhilarating of experiences. Matching or superceeding that experience is the dopamine rush as you anticipate the achievement. My son reminded me to leverage dopamine to achieve my goals at the weekend. A Wise 'Boy' Indeed. And so I visualised a Great day at work. Arriving full of optimism, then wadding through the corporate governance, policies, culture, bureaucracy, busy people in a fuss, to move the needle towards my objectives. Bruised, fatigued, bearly about to stand after the relentless onslaught of the day, you emerge from the office and fall to your knees in physical and emotional pain. Raise your hands to the sky to beg for forgiveness and you remember. "Tonight is Arms and Chest in the Gym". Back to visualisation, imagining lifting more than I have ever lifted before and smiling with the energy of a bear while doing it. The rush of dopamine enabling you to stand back on one foot, then the other. You strap your shield to your arm, place your sword on your back and finally wipe the blood from your face. Your back in the game for a second round of achievements this day ⚔️🐻💫

My life. I really am in love with my experiences. I feel the love of life.
