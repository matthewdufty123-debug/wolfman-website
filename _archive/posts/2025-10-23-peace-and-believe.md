## I'm Grateful For

Making a data visualisation that enables a decision to be made. So offen I see visualisations in Power BI, Tableau and other BI tools that looks real fancy but missed the mark on 'usefulness' scale. It's like you have given an artist free raine to build a bridge. It has twists and turns with cool colours everywhere. It looks amazing from the shore, but misses the point of a bridge. Cars are meant to drive across it and the designer omited a road... Visualising data is a fine balance between the art of design and function. We want to stand out as Analysts so we go all in on the Fancy. But sometimes, less is more. Let the visual drive a decision. Then, subtly, tart it up afterwards.
