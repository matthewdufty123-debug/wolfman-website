## Today's Intention

The mind is a beautiful sponge. Ready to absorb lots of great information. Sometimes it can get saturated, making it difficult to take in new liquid information. These times can be tough. Especially when your trying to learn something new. This week I've been trying to learn something new with a saturated mind. It's been really hard but over the past 24 hours I've managed to squeeze information liquid from my brain that is serving no real purpose. This morning I feel like I've got a nice dry brain ready to absorb some lovely new information that serves a great purpose. And with that I'll be doing some training today. Learning how to use a new technology (Databricks) and I'm really excited about it.

## I'm Grateful For

knowing when my brain is foggy and not focused. It used to take me a long time to realise I was distracted with things that were outside of my control. These days, I tend to catch it within a day or two. Then I can let go of stuff that doesn't really matter too much. Enabling focus on the stuff that does matter to me, my family, friends, work, pets, environment, the universe.
