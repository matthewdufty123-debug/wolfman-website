## Today's Intention

Finish some gosh darn tasks. So many tasks on the to-do list. This is amazing, but don't let the list get to big otherwise it will become a burden. Let's tick off some tasks today and have fun while do it. Remember, they don't need to be perfect. Just follow your gut and do what feels right for each task. Do it with love and whoever is the recipient, be it someone else, or yourself, will feel the love. Imagine how much love will circle today if we all take this approach.

## I'm Grateful For

My Mother. Tuesday is Mother's day. Every Tuesday is Mother's day. I wake, I work at home for a bit, I travel to Mothers at lunch. I continue work for a little bit then we take the dogs for a walk in the countryside and eat nice food. It's the perfect way to spend a Tuesday.

## I'm Grateful For

Trusting my gut and not over analysing. There are people in this world that need to analyse something to the minute detail before making a decision. I'm not that person. I see a goal and I follow my an instinctive path towards it. Whether it's the right or wrong decision is irrelevant. This is what's happening so let's enjoy, learn and adapt along the way. It's so easy to get bogged down in if something is the correct course of action. While you're in this state, you are in a state of No-Action, and no one likes No-Action. I prefer to start with Some-Action with the intention of making it the Best-Action.
