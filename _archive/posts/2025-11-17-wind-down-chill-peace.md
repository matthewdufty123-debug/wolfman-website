## Today's Intention

Wind down, chill, peace. My brain was/is so buzzed up after weekend with house work, exercise, adventure, people, conversations. It was amazing in so many different ways. When you align with it, it feels great. But I don't want to over indulge in all this 'Buzzed Up' feeling. So I'll bring a bit of Yin in today. Rebalance.

The conversations I have with the people I have in my life. The beauty of a conversation can not be underestimated. Unlocks area of the mind that lay dormant. Challenges you in a way you weren't expecting. Can change a mood from a frown to a smile. And evening make you a better person once the conversation is concluded.

I was just out and embracing the feeling. And, as a reward. The stars looked glorious this morning. The sky was super clear and I could see the universe in all it's glory.
