## Today's Intention

I walked outside at 3:30am to put my feet on the ground. Looked up and the Moon was shining down so beautiful. Moving onto the other side of the Moon is an amazing time for me. I feel like there is a energy on the build up to it that can feel chaotic. My brain tends to be jumping around a bit in a whirlwind of action. Once we step over the Full Moon I feel like all that Chaos comes into crystal clear focus. I felt that went I looked up this morning. At total peace and as clear as can be about my day ahead.

## I'm Grateful For

Well, let's just say it. The Moon brings us so much. Without it life on this planet with be completely different. I'm Greatful for the cycles of energy it brings and the tides. It's always fun seeing where the tide is when your at the beach.

## I'm Grateful For

Being patient when there is a bat flying around my home. The most exhilarating of experience is last night. A great big bat came into my home on the day of the Full Moon. Say I was startled is an understatement. I feared the bat at first, but then I remembered to embrace these experiences when they happen. So I sat in the lounge after opening the windows and watched him/her fly around until my body calmed down and I could enjoy the experience and then the bat disappeared out the window into the night. Pretty amazing stuff.
