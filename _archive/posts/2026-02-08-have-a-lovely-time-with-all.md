## I'm Grateful For

All the people I will interact with today.

Something in Great At; Configuration Management ( For any of you PRINCE2 dorks out there 🤓) Configuration Management underpins the success of many things we deliver. It's all about asset organisation. Can you reference something easily, can you find it, read it, know what it does. Cataloguing things might seem easy on paper, but the application of a configuration management strategy across a wide department can be quite the challenge. It's one of those things that when done right, know body notices. The naming conventions work, people can find things, they just get on with the tasks at hand. When things are done wrong... Oh gosh. People can't find stuff, it builds frustration, people go off and do their own things because they can find information. It can be the downfall of any project, programme or portfolio of work. 

So to increase the chances of successfully maintaining your asset library, and as any Project Management Office would tell you, You need to be very strong when it comes to configuration management. If you see someone has not documented properly, they have not used the correct naming convention, not saved stuff in the correct place, not used the correct sequencing... SORT IT OUT, QUICK!!! If you're in charge of configuration management, own the damn thing. You need to be the document police and police it well. Because documentation is a key pillar of success.

I love configuration management and I love being nitpicky about what might seem like the most mundane of things people. So here is to putting a underscore in the correct place. And if you have not done so, be ready for a reminder teams message ever 15 minutes, with a gif of someone tapping there watch, until it's fixed.
